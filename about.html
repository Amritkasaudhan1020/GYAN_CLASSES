About Us


Gyan Classes: Empowering Minds, Nurturing Excellence


At Gyan Classes, we believe in the transformative power of education. We are a renowned coaching center located in the heart of Nansa Bazar, dedicated to providing high-quality education and shaping the future of our students. With a strong focus on academic excellence, holistic development, and personalized attention, we have become the preferred choice for students seeking the best coaching experience in the area.


At Gyan Classes, we understand that each student is unique with distinct learning needs. Therefore, we maintain small batch sizes to ensure individualized attention and foster effective student-teacher interaction. Our experienced faculty members provide personalized guidance, address doubts and queries, and offer constructive feedback to help students overcome challenges and maximize their potential.


Our Mission

We, being the young and enthusiastic, have taken up the responsibility of educating the “Young India” and building a better educated world in a manner which ensures that every student achieves success at every sphere of their life. At our institute you can find the appropriate conglomeration of enthusiasm and experience which sets us aside from rest of the institutes.

What we do

Practical & Hand On Way Teaching

Real-life simulations and case studies for practical understanding. Interactive experiments and demonstrations for experiential learning. Problem-solving exercises and group activities to enhance critical thinking skills. Field trips and industry exposure for practical exposure. Project-based assignments to foster creativity and practical application. Practical workshops and guest lectures by industry experts for real-world insights

Special attention to weak students

We try to be a friend to the weak child. When a friendship is built, student starts loving his teacher and after that child obeys whatever his teacher says. Here mystery of student’s success lies in understanding and friendship between teacher and child.
